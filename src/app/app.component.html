Geriatric Depression Scale

This form is designed to assess feelings of depression in older adults. Please check off the statements that apply to you to help us understand your emotional well-being. If more than 5 checkboxes are selected, it may indicate signs of depression. Your responses are confidential and will assist in providing the appropriate support.
